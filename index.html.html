A Strategic Blueprint for Sustainable Growth

MyTelkomsel faces a critical challenge: a large but inconsistent user base. This interactive report outlines the 'MyTelkomsel Everyday' initiative, a three-pillar strategy to transform the app from a simple utility into an indispensable daily companion.

The Core Challenge: A "Leaky Bucket"

Despite a large user base, daily engagement is stagnant and a significant portion of users are inconsistent or lapse monthly. The goal is to bridge the gap and foster daily habits.

Daily Active Users (DAU)

The DAU has plateaued, requiring a +28% uplift to meet the year-end target of 20 million.

Monthly Active Users (MAU)

While MAU is growing, the base is unstable. The target is to solidify growth and reach 80 million users.

The "Leaky Bucket"

21.3% of users are inconsistent, and ~10% lapse monthly, indicating a weak emotional connection to the app.

The 'MyTelkomsel Everyday' Initiative

A synergistic three-pillar solution to reshape the user experience, making it more personal, rewarding, and communal. Click through the pillars to explore the key initiatives.

"For You, Every Day"

Shift from a generic portal to a hyper-personalized interface that anticipates user needs and makes every interaction relevant, timely, and effortless.

Dynamic, AI-Driven Homepage

A personalized feed that adapts based on user behavior, location, and time of day, surfacing the most relevant content and offers automatically.

Predictive & Proactive Offers

Analyze usage patterns to predict needs, pushing personalized data packages *before* a user runs out, improving experience and conversion.

Upgraded "Veronika" Assistant

Evolve the chatbot into a true agentic AI that can understand complex commands and execute multi-step tasks seamlessly.

'Telkomsel Quests' Ecosystem

Systematically build daily habits by applying proven game mechanics, turning every interaction into an act of progress toward a tangible reward.

Daily Check-in & Streaks

A low-friction mechanic to create a daily trigger, rewarding users for simply opening the app, with bonuses for consistency.

Tiered Quest System

Daily, weekly, and monthly quests that guide users to explore the full app ecosystem, from entertainment to lifestyle partners.

Revamped Tiered Loyalty

A visible, aspirational tier system (Bronze, Silver, Gold) with exclusive benefits, making loyalty a tangible status symbol.

Interactive Progress Visuals

Engaging progress bars and animations to enhance motivation and provide a satisfying sense of accomplishment.

'MyTsel Stories' Community Hub

Build a vibrant in-app community that drives organic engagement, provides powerful social proof, and forges a deep emotional bond with the brand.

#MyTselEveryday UGC Campaign

Recurring, themed contests (e.g., #MyTselTravel) that encourage users to share their experiences, generating authentic, on-brand content.

In-App Community Hub

A dedicated tab to showcase user submissions where others can browse, vote, and comment, creating a compelling reason to return daily.

Integrated Reviews & Testimonials

Incentivize users to review services and partners, building trust and providing invaluable real-time feedback on the ecosystem.
